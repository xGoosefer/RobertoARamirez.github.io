Tools Used: Mega Man Maker

Made: September 2023

Lost Castle is a short action platformer level made using mega man maker.

This is another solo project i worked on for about a week. It was a fun experiment to try out mega man maker for the first time. I had fun figuring out what enemy types to use and what art assets to incorporate, provided by mega man maker, to keep a sort of theme going throughout the level.

I wanted to make a medieval themed level for mega man. I didn't know much about mega man when i started, so i was very much surprised when i found a lot of assets in the engine that allowed me to make this work. I wanted to make a unique level layout where the player would start right outside the castle, then traverse the insides to where they reach the rooftop (midway point of the level), and finally descend down to finish the latter half of the castle and defeat the boss waiting for them at the end.
